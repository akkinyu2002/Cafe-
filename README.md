# Welcome to the Kanigiri Cafe Website!

Hey there! 🌟 We're so excited to introduce you to our Kanigiri Cafe restaurant website. This little corner of the web is designed to give you a taste of what we offer in our cozy cafe. Below is a friendly overview of our code structure and features. We hope you enjoy it as much as we do!

## HTML Structure
Our HTML files are organized to provide a clear layout for our pages. Here's how we structured them:
- **index.html**: This is the heart of our website. It contains the main layout and links to all other pages.
- **menu.html**: Here you'll find the delicious menu items we offer, complete with descriptions and images.
- **about.html**: A little history about our cafe, the team, and our mission. 
- **contact.html**: Want to reach us? This page has all the info you need to get in touch!

## CSS Styling
We love to keep things stylish! Our CSS files help bring the website to life:
- **styles.css**: This is our main stylesheet. It controls the layout, colors, fonts, and overall aesthetics of the site.
- **responsive.css**: Making sure everyone can enjoy our site on any device is super important. This stylesheet adapts our layout for mobile and tablet views.

## JavaScript Features
To make our website interactive and fun, we've added some JavaScript goodness:
- **app.js**: The brains of our operations! This handles things like form submissions, animations, and any fun effects that enhance your experience.
- **menu.js**: This script dynamically updates the menu based on user interactions, making it easy for you to explore our offerings!

## Features to Explore
- **Responsive Design**: Whether you're on a phone or laptop, our site looks great everywhere! 📱💻
- **Dynamic Menu**: Our menu updates smoothly without needing to refresh the page.
- **Contact Form**: Got questions? Send them our way via the contact form, and we'll get back to you!

Thanks for visiting our website's code! We can't wait for you to experience everything Kanigiri Cafe has to offer. Happy browsing! 😊
