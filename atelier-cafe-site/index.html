Our Story

Built around the pace of old Hyderabad evenings.

Aster & Ember started with one question: what if a cafe felt like a private living room, and a restaurant felt like an open kitchen at home? The answer became this space, where every table receives the same care as the first one of the day.

Our pastry bench starts before sunrise, our spice blends are toasted in small batches, and our coffee program changes with each harvest.

House Values

  • Seasonal ingredients first
  • Small-batch roasting and baking
  • Service that feels thoughtful, never loud
